Smart Organ Donation App (SODA)

What is SODA?

SODA is a Smart Organ Donation web app that supports the infrastructure for an organ donation network in local hospitals that can be implemented in rural situations.

Why SODA?

In many countries, there are issues where there is no infrastructure to organize organ donations and manage the information of patients in a way that is also accessible to Alexa.
